Accommodation

Find the best hotels, hostels, and apartments in Madrid for all budgets and preferences.

  • Hotel Riu Plaza España: Rooftop views and central location.
  • The Hat Madrid: Modern and budget-friendly hostel experience.

Madrid offers a wide range of accommodation options, from luxury hotels to affordable apartments. Book in advance during peak seasons (spring and autumn) to get the best rates.

Restaurants

Discover Madrid's delicious cuisine in traditional and modern restaurants.

  • Casa Botín: The world's oldest restaurant, famous for its roast suckling pig.
  • Mercado de San Miguel: A variety of tapas in one place.

Madrid's culinary scene is diverse and exciting. Don't miss traditional dishes like cocido madrileño, callos a la madrileña, and tapas in La Latina.

Transportation

Get around the city easily using Madrid's extensive and efficient public transportation system.

  • Madrid Tourist Travel Pass: Unlimited public transportation for tourists.
  • BiciMAD: The city's shared bike service.

Madrid's metro is one of the most comprehensive in Europe. You can also use city buses and commuter trains to explore the city and its surroundings.

Guided Tours

Join guided tours to learn about Madrid's history, art, and culture with local experts.

  • Free walking tour of the historic center
  • Guided tour of the Prado Museum

Guided tours are an excellent way to discover Madrid's secrets. Many offer themed options like tapas tours, street art tours, or nighttime walks.

Shopping

Discover Madrid's shopping areas, from luxury boutiques to artisanal markets.

  • Gran Vía: Fashion from major brands
  • El Rastro: Famous Sunday flea market

Madrid is a paradise for shopping lovers. Don't miss Serrano Street for luxury brands or Malasaña for vintage shops and independent designers.

Emergency Services

Contact information and locations for hospitals, police stations, and other emergency services.

  • General emergency number: 112
  • Hospital Universitario La Paz: One of the city's top hospitals

In case of a medical emergency, you can also visit any public health center. For assistance in English, call 112 and request an English-speaking operator.
