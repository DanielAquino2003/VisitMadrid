Frequently Asked Questions

You can get free Madrid maps at the Sol Tourism Center or any of the city's tourist information points. We also offer downloadable digital maps on our website.

The Madrid Tourist Travel Pass is available at metro stations, tourism offices, and some city kiosks. You can also purchase it online through our official website.

Madrid is beautiful year-round, but spring (April-May) and autumn (September-October) offer pleasant temperatures and fewer tourists. Summer can be very hot, while winter is cool but filled with holiday charm.
